    • Admission Requirements

    Detailed list of documents and items required for admission to Uhuru Secondary School

    General Requirements

    • Certified copy of Birth Certificate
    • KCPE result slip certified by former head teacher
    • Two passport size photographs (not from 'photo me studios'), with name printed at back and signed by former head teacher
    • School Leaving Certificate signed and stamped by former primary school head
    • Medical certificate duly filled by a qualified doctor
    • ID copies of Parents/Guardians
    • Fees (per 2023 Fees Structure)
    • Two (2) Colored Passport Photographs for Parents/Guardians/Sponsor

    Boarding Requirements

    • A towel
    • Underwear (at least 5 pairs)
    • Toothpaste and toothbrush
    • 2 pairs of black laced-up leather shoes
    • 1 pair of white rubber shoes
    • Washing and bathing soap (enough for one term)
    • Tin trunk with name printed and good padlocks
    • Toilet paper (at least 10 for the term)
    • A spoon
    • Two good padlocks for desk and locker
    • A pair of slippers
    • A metal bucket/pail (big size)
    • End of term bus fare
    • Pocket money (Maximum Kshs.1,000/- per term) to be paid in cash to Acc. Office
    • School bag (reasonable size for day-to-day use)

    Stationery and Books

    • Geometrical set
    • 30cm transparent ruler
    • 4-figure Mathematics table (6th edition)
    • Spring file
    • Blue biro pens and pencils
    • Oxford Advanced Learner's Dictionary
    • Kamusi ya Kiswahili Sanifu (TUKI)
    • A Rebirth (OUP)
    • Heri Subira (OUP) (Class reader)
